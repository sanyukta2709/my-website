About Me

Strategic Finance Leader
with a Creative Edge

With over 15 years of experience spanning infrastructure, cloud, and customer-success finance, I bring a rare combination of analytical rigour and creative problem-solving to every organisation I serve. My career has taken me across some of the world’s most admired companies — Microsoft, Amazon AWS, Expedia, UBS, and Tata Consultancy Services — building deep expertise in FP&A, controllership, cost optimisation, and revenue strategy.

I believe the best financial decisions live at the intersection of data and imagination. Whether I’m designing a new revenue metric, leading a multi-hundred-million-dollar CapEx portfolio, or mentoring a cross-functional team, I show up with the same energy: curious, structured, and relentlessly focused on outcomes.
